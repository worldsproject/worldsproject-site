What is a Seed Factory?

A seed factory is a special type of factory that focus on three main elements: self-expansion, integration, and automation. A fully capable seed factory is, in theory, able to produce any product, including copies of itself.

How does WorldsProject plan on building a Seed Factory?

Instead of directly attacking the problem head on, such as other projects, WorldsProject plans to build the tools and software that can be reused in a Seed Factory setting. The inital goal is to better automate and decentralize food production, and apply lessons learned to other forms of manufacturing.
